Soon to be a variety of scripts and tools for analysing dispersal, originally
developed to understand seed dispersal in plant populations.


pmiDiversity.R

Defines the R function pmiDiversity() which takes a site-by-source table and
produces statistics for Probability of Maternal Identity aka PMI (Grivet et al.
2005 Mol Ecol, Scofield et al. 2010 J Ecol) and dispersal diversity (Scofield
et al. in review).
